About Stitched Ridese

Founded in 2008, Stitched Ridese has been providing top-quality automotive repair services to our community for over 15 years. Our team of ASE-certified technicians is committed to delivering honest, reliable service you can trust.

We believe in transparency and customer education. That's why we take the time to explain every repair and maintenance service in detail, so you can make informed decisions about your vehicle.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest diagnostic tools and technology to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible.
